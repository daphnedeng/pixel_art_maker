    Main Ingredients

  • nature Salmon, green onion

    • Secondery Ingredients

  • nature Salt, oil, soy sauce, or any seasoning of you choice

    Difficulity

  • bubble_chart Elemantary

    • Cooking Time

  • timelapse 10 Minutes

1.  Preheat grill on high heat. After grill is heated, clean grate with grill brush. Reduce heat to medium high heat.

2.  Generously oil the grill grate by squirting some vegetable oil on a paper towel and using grill tongs, rub the oil on the grill grate.

3.  Season salmon fillets with salt and pepper. Place salmon diagonally on the grill grate. Grill salmon for 3-4 minutes per inch of thickness on each side with the lid closed.
